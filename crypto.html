Overview of Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrencies are digital or virtual currencies that leverage cryptographic techniques to secure transactions and control the creation of new units. Unlike traditional currencies, which are regulated by central authorities, cryptocurrencies operate on decentralized networks based on blockchain technology...

Why People Use Cryptocurrencies

There are several reasons why people are increasingly adopting cryptocurrencies:

  • Decentralization and Control: Cryptocurrencies provide users with more control over their assets...
  • Financial Inclusion: In regions where banking infrastructure is weak or inaccessible...
  • Security and Privacy: Cryptocurrencies are secured by cryptographic protocols...
  • Lower Transaction Costs: Traditional financial systems often charge fees for transfers...
  • Hedge Against Inflation: In countries where inflation is rampant...
  • Innovation and Investment: Cryptocurrencies represent an entirely new asset class...
